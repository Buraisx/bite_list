Our Vision

To help reduce surplus food waste by providing hungry customers with a convenient and affordable solution.

Affordable

Food items will be heavily discounted regularly, to give you the best and freshest deals so you don’t have to worry about breaking the bank.

Accessible

Find and connect with restaurants, bakeries and food shops closest to you, here’s a great chance to try something new!

Environmental

Eat more and feel even better, help tackle the food waste crisis by saying yes to surplus food and no to food waste!
